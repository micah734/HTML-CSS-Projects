All about Sloths

What do you know about sloths?

Picture of a Sloth

Sloths are 3 times stronger than us. Most sloths are blind and have an excellent sense of smell. Sloths can take 30 days for them to digest a leaf. Most interestingly, they can starve to death while having a full stomach. Sloths also can fall 100 feet without injury.

Here is a quote from GlobalAnimal.org

Sloths are adorable, slow-moving mammals native to Central and South America. They are solitary mammals and just so happen to provide a habitat for a multitude of other organisms. For instance, did you know a single sloth may be home to moths, beetles, cockroaches, fungi, and algae?
